Roadmap
=======

Near-term priorities
--------------------

- Calibration UX hardening (manual annotation and window lifecycle).
- Better synchronization diagnostics (timestamps, drift summaries).
- Capture tooling around calibration data collection.

Planned third-party conversion
------------------------------

Third-party calibration conversion is planned for future releases.

Planned formats include:

- Qualisys
- Vicon
- OpenCap
- EasyMocap
- bioCV

These conversion paths are roadmap items and are not part of the current stable feature set.

Longer-term
-----------

- Pairwise extrinsics chaining and global optimization.
- Hardware-assisted synchronization options.
- Expanded device backends and richer diagnostics.
